DASHR — FREE BROWSER PLATFORMER

DashR is a free online platformer game playable directly in your browser — no download, no install. Master running, jumping, dashing and wall-jumping to conquer 10 increasingly difficult levels across two distinct worlds: the Meadow and the Iron Caverns.

HOW TO PLAY

Use your keyboard to control your character. Master the dash move to cross large gaps and chain wall-jumps to scale vertical obstacles. Reach the goal gem at the end of each level.

← →   Move left / right
Z / Space   Jump
X   Dash (8 directions)
Wall + ↑   Wall-jump
R   Restart level
Esc   Back to menu

FEATURES

DashR features a full title screen with customizable controls, audio settings, and a character dressing room where you can change your player's color. The game tracks your best time per level, total deaths and cumulative run time — perfect for speedrunning challenges.

Two distinct visual themes — green prairie and dark caverns — each with unique decorations, enemy behaviors, moving platforms, and teleport portals in the later levels. The game runs entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, with no external dependencies.
