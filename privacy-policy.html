PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the website tyra667.github.io ("the Site"), operated by Tyra667, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit and play DashR.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not directly collect any personal information from visitors. The game DashR runs entirely in your browser and does not send any game data to a server. No account creation or login is required.

However, third-party services used on this site may collect data as described below.

2. GOOGLE ADSENSE

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to display ads that are relevant to your interests, based on your browsing history across websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps .

3. COOKIES

This site itself does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party advertisers (Google AdSense) may place cookies on your device for the purpose of ad personalization and measurement. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

To opt out of interest-based advertising from Google, you can also use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool or the Your Online Choices tool (for European users).

4. GITHUB PAGES

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses and browser type for security and performance purposes. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Since we do not collect personal data directly, most rights relate to data held by our third-party partners (Google). You can exercise your rights regarding Google's data through your Google Account.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach the site owner through the GitHub profile.
